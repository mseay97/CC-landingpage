Credit Cards! Travel Hacking 101
Traveling can cost thousands of dollars. I am here to help you make those trips cost a fraction of the price! Using this guide, you can save thousands of dollars on airfare and hotels. How can we do this? Credit Card Points!
The many uses of Credit Card points
Airfare! Transferring points to an airline partner is a great way to save hundreds of dollars on flights
Hotels! Transferring points to a hotel partner can score you free nights on your accommodation when booking with certain hotel partners
Luxury Stays! All inclusive resorts are some of the best way to transfer those credit cards points to partners!
Cruises! Not widley known, but You can even transfer credit card points to certain cruise lines for all inclusive cruises!
